FAQ

Got questions?

How do you tailor Google Ads strategies for different practices?+
We start with a deep audit of your local market, competitor activity, and patient acquisition costs. Then we build campaigns around the specific procedures, treatments, and patient demographics that drive your highest-LTV bookings — never a one-size-fits-all template.
What's unique about your Meta Ads approach?+
Performance creative built to your audience — never templated, always tested against real metrics. We run a hook bank of 12+ ad concepts each month and let the data pick the winners, then we double down on what books.
How do you handle content production?+
We hand you a complete monthly content plan — scripts, shot lists, lighting and camera angles, captions, and edits. Then we either produce on-site, or guide your team to film 30+ pieces in a single day with our director-led system.
How do you choose ad formats and placements across platforms?+
We optimize against booked consultations, not clicks or leads. The platform mix is dynamic — most months we run Meta + Google, but we'll fold in YouTube or TikTok if your patient demo lives there.
What reporting and analytics do you provide?+
Weekly dashboards that surface the only metric that matters — qualified consultations booked. Plus monthly strategy calls to review what's working, what's not, and where the next 30 days of testing is headed.
Do you offer ongoing optimization and management?+
Yes — every retainer includes daily campaign optimization, weekly creative testing, monthly strategic reviews, and 24/7 Slack access to your dedicated account team.
